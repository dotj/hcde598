Assignment 7: Project Proposal

Concept

I wanted to revisit the lamp assignment since I didn't get to spend as much time on it as I wanted to. When looking for inspiration, I was drawn to the spherical lamps with some interesting geometric form. I plan to make a simple mechanism to rotate the lampshades (cut and set in 2 layers) to make interesting patterns. I'm also sticking to easily accessible materials. :)

Tasks

  1. Design spinny mechanism
    • Will be designed in Onshape.
    • This mechanism should be the same regardless of the final shape of the lamp so it can hopefully be done independently.
  2. Build spinny mechanism
    • Can be prototyped in cardboard.
    • Delrin will be laser cut and/or CNC milled.
  3. Design Lampshade
    • Will be designed in Grasshopper/Rhino.
    • Hopefully will be a sphere, fallback will be a simple cylinder. Will either go for a monochrome + geometric design or with colored paper.
  4. Build Lampshade
    • Fix mechanism to lamp.
    • Fix mechanism to lampshade (probably with some sort of glue.
